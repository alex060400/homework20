Research

Research was conducted using a combination of surveys, interviews, and analysis. The inital research process consisted of conducting user interviews. Test subjects were given a series of tasks and questions, gaining further insight into the boba tea market and One Milk Tea brand. Users clicked through the menu, and described any painpoints within their experience. We used these interviews as a way to gauge what users wanted and felt needed improvement within the current One Milk Tea site. Subsequent research consisted of competitor analysis, google surveys, and user personas.
