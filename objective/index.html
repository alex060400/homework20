Problem Statement

The rapid expansion of the One Milk Tea franchise has seen the pressing need to update and streamline the website to cater to a wide array of users. Through our research and user tests we have found that the lack of pictures and structure on the One Milk Tea menu and homepage somewhat hurt the user experience. Reorganizing the homepage and menu is key, in doing so customers can have an easier time ordering thus leading to a better experience in the shop.
